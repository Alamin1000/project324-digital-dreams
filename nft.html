Our strategy for NFT creations

The approach with NFTs works by creating a unique digital asset and then assigning it a unique token ID on a blockchain. This token ID serves as a digital certificate of ownership for the asset, which can be bought, sold, and traded just like physical assets. The value of an NFT is determined by various factors, such as the rarity, uniqueness, and desirability of the asset it represents, as well as the demand for it on the marketplace.
